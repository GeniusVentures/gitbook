### OPEX Comparison

<table data-header-hidden><thead><tr><th valign="top"></th><th valign="top"></th><th valign="top"></th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td valign="top">Metric</td><td valign="top">Centralized xAI Cluster</td><td valign="top">GNUS.ai Decentralized Network</td></tr><tr><td valign="top">Compute Payment (Hourly)</td><td valign="top">$1.75 per chip × 100,000 chips ≈ $175,000 per hour</td><td valign="top">$0.005 per node × ~6.7 million nodes ≈ $33,500 per hour</td></tr><tr><td valign="top">Annual Compute Cost</td><td valign="top">~$1.53 billion per year</td><td valign="top">~$293 million per year (paid directly to node operators)</td></tr><tr><td valign="top">Electricity &#x26; Cooling</td><td valign="top">High estimated ~$61 million per year for electricity alone</td><td valign="top">Borne by individual node operators (minimal impact on GNUS)</td></tr><tr><td valign="top">Network &#x26; Storage Costs</td><td valign="top">Additional expenses (~$100 million/year)</td><td valign="top">Minimal—local connectivity and distributed storage</td></tr><tr><td valign="top">Total Annual OPEX</td><td valign="top">Approximately $1.7 billion per year</td><td valign="top">On the order of $2–$10 million per year (true OPEX for coordination)</td></tr></tbody></table>

_Note:_ For GNUS.ai, the $293 million figure represents the total “compute payment” distributed in tokens, which is a pass–through payment to node operators and not an overhead cost for the GNUS network itself.
